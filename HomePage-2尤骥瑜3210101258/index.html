How I move the project forward
First master the background information, collect materials, and then combine the materials for thinking storm, and then simplify the design on the basis of the existing.
Step1:Problem definition
Accurately define the problem, clarify the scope, objectives and constraints of the problem, as well as the various factors and relationships designed for the problem.
Step2:Brainstorming
Use heuristic thinking, brainstorming, simulation and other methods and tools to stimulate the generation and divergence of ideas to solve problems or meet needs.
Step3:Prototyping
Make low-fidelity prototypes with guaranteed core functions at a lower cost to verify the feasibility of the idea and serve as the basis for iteration.
Step4:Model iteration
Based on the test feedback of the prototype, several optimization iterations are carried out, and finally a complete high-fidelity model is formed.

Environmentally Friendly Graphic Work
Design comes from nature and changes nature.
Forest decline
This one uses the pine tree icon and compares it to a match. Matches are perishable and can be burned up easily. The black and red color scheme is like a warning to people that burning matches is dangerous and it tells people that destroying forests is dangerous.
Biodiversity reduction
Choose to use common animal silhouetts to refer to biodiversity, and use the extinction of very common things to remind people of the terror of human power, mainly to warn people not to think that a certain thing is wanton killing, resulting in the reduction of biodiversity.
Land salinization
Land salinization will cause the plant to burn, and this is a slow process, so the image of mosquito-repellent incense is used below, and land salinization will seriously affect the food problem, thus affecting people's lives, so the image of grass seedlings is used in the upper part.
Global warming
The idea is that the most obvious manifestation of global warming is the melting of glaciers, here talking about the melting of icebergs, analogous to the melting of candles, alert people that icebergs are like candles, melting and gone, leaving behind is the candle tears, that is, the tears of repentance.
